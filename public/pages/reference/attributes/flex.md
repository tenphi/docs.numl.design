The **flex** property sets how a flex item will grow or shrink to fit the space available in its flex container. It is a shorthand for [grow](./grow.md), [shrink](./shrink.md), and [basis](./basis.md).

## Syntax

```
[ <'grow'> <'shrink'>? || <'basis'> ]
```
